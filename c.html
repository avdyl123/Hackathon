How Travel Empowers Local Communities

Discover the ways tourism can create sustainable jobs and improve local economies while respecting cultural heritage.

Tourism, when done responsibly, supports local economies by creating jobs in areas like hospitality, guiding, and handicrafts. Communities can thrive while preserving traditions and environment. Sustainable travel encourages respect and long-term benefits for locals.

Supporting People with Special Needs Through Inclusive Tourism

Learn about inclusive travel initiatives that ensure accessibility and dignity for travelers with special needs worldwide.

Inclusive tourism projects focus on removing barriers for travelers with disabilities. This includes accessible transport, accommodations, and attractions. Such efforts promote dignity and freedom, allowing all to enjoy travel equally.
