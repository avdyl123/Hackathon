Our Mission

At InclusiveWork, we believe everyone deserves the opportunity to grow, contribute, and thrive. Our mission is to empower individuals with special needs by providing meaningful employment, skills training, and a supportive work environment. Thanks to partners like TravelNow, 20% of their bookings go directly to support our programs.

What We Do

  • Job Placement: We match candidates with inclusive employers.
  • Skills Training: We offer workshops in tech, crafts, customer service, and more.
  • Workplace Support: On-site support staff to assist with onboarding and daily tasks.
